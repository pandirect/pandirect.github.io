Interactive medical testing

A web application for testing doctors in a gamified format.

Industry

Medical

Client

HardDays

Project Type

Web Application

The Challenge

Our partner, HardDays, approached us with an urgent need: they had an upcoming deadline for their innovative medical testing platform, DocClub, but had no frontend prepared. They needed a dedicated team to build it from the ground up, quickly and efficiently.

Our Solution

Pandirect immediately allocated a specialized frontend team to collaborate with HardDays. Our agile approach allowed us to deliver functionality in stages, starting with core features and iterating based on continuous feedback.

Key achievements included:

  • Development of unique gamified elements, including a card game, a quiz module, and a medical case simulator.
  • Implementation of internationalization (i18n) to support both English and Portuguese, preparing the app for a global audience.
  • Maintained flexibility to adapt to evolving requirements and design updates throughout the project.

The Results

Despite the tight timeline and evolving scope, the project was delivered ahead of schedule, minimizing risks and ensuring a successful launch.

  • DocClub was successfully launched in both Brazil and the United States.
  • Pandirect managed the entire frontend development lifecycle, creating a gamified, engaging, and globally-ready platform that met all client expectations.
